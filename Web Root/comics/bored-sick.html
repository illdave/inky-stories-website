Bored Sick

(PG; Memoir) Story by Dana Marie Andra (formerly Mark Burbey), art by David Marshall. A pair of adult siblings react very differently to childhood trauma. One becomes a serial killer. Published in Street Music, an anthology by Fantagraphics. Selected pages presented here in "artist edition" format.
