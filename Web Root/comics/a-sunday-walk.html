A Sunday Walk

(PG; Drama) Story by Dana Marie Andra (formerly Mark Burbey), art by Dave Marshall. While strolling through a San Francisco park on a lovely day, two people encounter their lost memories, hidden egos and unspoken desires. Published in Street Music, a drama anthology by Fantagraphic Books.
