Inky Stories at Agencyport: Opening Reception

Over 30 pages of my comic book art are on display in Boston's vibrant and creative Fort Point Channel. Please join us for the opening reception on August 8th.

David Marshall July 25th, 2013 March 4th, 2020

Gallery @ Agencyport is the art exhibition space of Agencyport Software. As part of the historic Fort Point Channel creative community, it promotes the work of local artists with monthly exhibitions. For some reason, they decided to hang my work for a month! See for yourself at the opening reception:

Opening Reception for Inky Stories at Agencyport
Thursday, August 8, 2013
5:00pm–8:00pm
51 Sleeper Street, 8th Floor (Google Map)
Boston MA 02210 | 866-539-6623
One block from Boston Children's Museum
MBTA: South Station

Original ink-on-paper art (and a few high-quality prints) of complete short stories ( "Six-Year-Old Horse Thief", "Bottle of Red" and "Twilight’s Last Gleaming") as well as one-page wonders "Grubhub Delivery", "Go Snow!", "Ladybat and the Fanboy", "Plate Ground" and "The Million Dollar Debut of Batgirl".

The questionable art will be offset by great food, libations and company. Can't wait to see you there.

David Marshall
