Commissions rate sheet

Prices for my studio commissions, convention sketches, and original art

David Marshall April 3, 2025 April 5, 2025
Help me spend more time drawing!

These prices here are for the most popular commission requests. Out-of-the-box ideas like alternative paper sizes, color, story or writing can be negotiated during our first contact.

  • UPS shipping is $20 per shipment (save if you order more!)
  • Sizes measured in inches, prices in US dollars
  • All art is black and white
  • I don't take art direction of any kind

Convention Sketches

These are drawn with marker on sketch paper. Walk-in sketch commissions are cool, but the waiting list fills up fast and are accepted on a first-come, first-serve basis. Pre-order by email to save time. Everyone who orders and pays in advance gets a free print with any order of $100 or more.
