Free comics art lessons @ Hub Comics

All-ages art activity on Free Comic Book Day
Hub Comics
19 Bow Street, Somerville MA 02143 (directions)
617-718-0987
Saturday May 2, 2009
Noon–5:00pm

Details

As part of this year's Free Comic Book Day, local comic book artist Dave Marshall (hey, that's me!) will give free demonstrations of professional ink-on-paper techniques for making comic books. Open to all ages. The demos are planned as a loop of 30-minute sessions, but may run over due to popular demand. Participants are encouraged to bring their own art supplies or use the instructor's during the closing Q&A.
