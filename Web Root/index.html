Commissions open!

Let's make something extraordinary.

My current schedule allows time for art requests. I work with traditional ink-on-paper media, hunched over an Anco Bilt drafting table like the old school masters. Art, lettering, and design are all done by hand. Capturing the authentic feel of vintage comics in current times with era-appropriate tools has been my passion for over 20 years. Commissions include character portraits, pin-ups, model sheets, pitch samples, and faux cover recreations. Let's create something extraordinary.
