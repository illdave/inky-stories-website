Agent K and the Null Device G PG13

These stories take place in The Null Device, a recently rebooted universe built on UNIX and meta-level comic book history.

The comics

Agent K is an independent adventurer sometimes known as Karen Taschen. She collects amazing friends and powerful enemies while trying to find her father the Director, a former programmer who rebooted the universe.

Stop That Panzer Woman! Agent K's first solo mission: stop an alien invasion by going deep undercover in a UNIX war zone.
The Null Device A reluctant hero gets deep into enemy territory in a UNIX war zone.
Enter...General Zaq! Anything's possible in the Silver Age Zone.
Cupid’s Got A Death Ray! Agent K tries to stop an army of alien killer robots from conquering Earth.
Agent K Judo Trick: The Strangler Karen teaches the art of self defense in 6-inch heels.
El Rey Day: Part 1 Karen's galactic search for her father uncovers local evil in an unearthly village.
Robots of Blood! Mecañica risks everything to protect her unearthly village from what looks like an alien invasion.
Quasara into the Fletcherverse WORK IN PROGRESS: Quasara against a mad scientist and his fire-breathing bat-winged demon shrimps.
