Commissions

Keep me off the streets and out of trouble by commissioning custom art! Together we can create the extraordinary.

I love to draw...for you!

Following the footsteps of old school legends, I'm usually hunched over an Anco Bilt drafting table, slapping ink on bristol with sable brushes, dip pens and calligraphy nibs. Whether you're looking for a character pin-up, heavy meta concept, or full-blown cover recreation, let's collaborate on bringing your vision into reality.
