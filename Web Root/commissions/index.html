Commission Art

Hand-Crafted Comic Art in the Classic Tradition

I love to draw...for you!

I work with traditional ink-on-paper media, hunched over an Anco Bilt drafting table like the old school masters. Art, lettering, and design are all done by hand. Capturing the authentic feel of vintage comics in current times with era-appropriate tools has been my passion for over 20 years. Commissions include character portraits, pin-ups, model sheets, pitch samples, and faux cover recreations. Fill out the commission request form below to start creating something extraordinary.
